Terms and Conditions

Please read our policies carefully before enrolling in our courses

Payment Policy

A 100% fee needs to be paid before starting the classes for any course.

Property Responsibility

Should a student cause any damage to the institution's property, they are to be held responsible for compensation including full costs of repair.

Attendance Policy

Students are expected to observe and act upon the class timetable and must attend all classes on time.

Refund Policy

Once the fee is paid, it will not be refunded.

Attendance and Fee Lapse Policy

After making the payment if the student doesn't attend any class for a month, then the fees paid would get lapsed and further students need to make repayment of a fee to take the classes again.

Installment Payment Policy

If a course fee is broken into an installment with mutual understanding of both parties. Then installment needs to be paid on or before the decided deadline, in case the student fails to make the payment of the installment on the decided date then 10% of the whole fee would be charged extra to the student.

Course Stack Change Policy

Once the admission has been done for a particular stack of courses will not be able to change the stack further. Appropriate permission and reason have to be quoted for change in a stack of the course.

Leave Policy

The student has to inform in prior if he/she is taking leave for a particular period.

Dress Code Policy

Students should wear clean, neat, and ironed clothes, combed hair, and perfumed.

Academic Project Policy

Academic projects once finalized cannot be changed or withdrawn, incase students want to withdraw the project amount paid will not be refund.
